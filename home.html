Go Draw!

Description:

This is a drawing app that uses a webcam and Tensorflow to track a user's hand positions.


In the future, we will be using Doodlenet to predict what the user has drawn.

We are also thinking of changing it so it uses your pointer finger instead of wrist, and are currently trying to flip the camera.

How to:

Draw with your hands! (Or mouse)


Using the camera, use your right hand to draw to the screen. Adjust color, width, and tool from the toolbar.

You can also clear or export the image from the toolbar.
